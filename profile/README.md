# Welcome to Bytez3

We are a Web3 Agency specializing in multi-chain code, audited contracts, and hybrid NFT utilities. We believe in building beyond the hype to deliver secure, scalable, and high-performance solutions.

## Our Repositories: Public vs. Private

To maintain strict confidentiality for our clients and protect proprietary systems, the vast majority of our work is maintained in **Private Repositories**.

### 🔒 Private Repositories (Client & Core Work)
Our core products and bespoke client solutions are completely hidden from public view. These include, but are not limited to:
*   **Bookeeper**: A robust, multi-tenant SaaS architecture and administrative dashboard.
*   **SolOnChain**: Extensive Solana ecosystem tools including a launchpad, bonding curves, and marketplace infrastructure.
*   **Aegiz**: On-Chain ML Threat Detection and wallet reputation systems.
*   **DexPrivacy / Aegiz Wallet**: A browser extension wallet powered by MagicBlock shielded swaps.
*   **Custom Game Engines**: E.g., The *Husks* on-chain INT8 neural network combat engine.
*   **Custom Staking Vaults**: Legacy, pNFT, and Core MetaPlex token standards.

If you are a client looking for your project, please refer to the private repository link provided directly by our team.

### 📖 Public Repositories (SDKs & Open Source)
We actively contribute back to the Web3 community by open-sourcing our SDKs and hackathon templates. Here you will find:
*   **[husks-sdk](https://github.com/Bytez3/husks-sdk)**: TypeScript SDK for the Husks on-chain AI auto-battler.
*   **[aegiz-sdk](https://github.com/Bytez3/aegiz-sdk)**: On-Chain AI Threat Detection SDK for Solana.
*   **Boilerplates & Templates**: Hackathon starters, Discord bots, and DeFi toolings (e.g., MEDiscordBuySell, v4-periphery forks).
